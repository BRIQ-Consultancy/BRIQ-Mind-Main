Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains the principles governing the processing and protection of personal data and your rights when using BRIQ Mind's digital products and services. BRIQ Mind attaches great importance to user privacy and undertakes to ensure the security of personal data in accordance with applicable legislation (primarily Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data—KVKK—and, where applicable, the GDPR).

1. Scope

This policy covers the processing of personal data collected through the briqmind.com domain and subdomains, APIs connected to this site, integrations, customer service, and technical support channels. The privacy policies of third‑party service providers apply separately.

  • Web platform: briqmind.com and subpages
  • APIs and integrations (e.g., form handling, email services)
  • Customer service and technical support communication channels

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Special Category Personal Data: Data such as race, ethnic origin, political opinion, health, biometric data, and similar.
  • Data Controller: The natural or legal person who determines the purposes and means of processing (BRIQ Mind).
  • Data Processor: A third party/entity that processes personal data on behalf of the data controller in line with its instructions.
  • Data Subject/Individual: The natural person whose personal data is processed.
  • Explicit Consent: Consent that is informed, specific, and freely given for a particular matter.

4. Categories of Data Collected

  • Identity Data: Name, surname, (if any) username.
  • Contact Data: Email, phone, company name (e.g., via contact forms).
  • Transaction and Session Data: IP address, browser and device information, operating system, date/time stamps, referrer page.
  • Behavioral Data: Page views, clicks and interaction logs, features used, search history.
  • Identifiers (Cookies): Session management, remembering preferences, performance measurement.
  • Content Data: Content you upload/submit (forms) and attachments.

5. Methods and Sources of Collection

  • Directly from you: Forms, email, support requests, contractual processes.
  • Automated means: Cookies, browser/device logs, security logs.
  • From third parties: Services we integrate with (only to the extent necessary).

6. Purposes of Processing

  • Respond to user requests; manage support and customer relationships.
  • Provide, maintain, improve, and personalize our services.
  • Ensure system security; prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Conduct analytics and reporting; assess performance, usage, and trends.
  • Fulfill legal obligations and respond to requests from competent authorities.
  • Send marketing and announcement communications (only with your consent; you may withdraw at any time).
  • Test and optimize our AI models using anonymized/synthetic data.

7. Legal Bases

Your personal data may be processed on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Explicit consent (KVKK Art. 5/1, GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Necessity for the establishment/performance of a contract (KVKK Art. 5/2(c), GDPR Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Legal obligation (KVKK Art. 5/2(ç), GDPR Art. 6(1)(c))
  • Legitimate interest (KVKK Art. 5/2(f), GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)), provided that your fundamental rights and freedoms are not harmed
  • Establishment, exercise or protection of rights (KVKK Art. 5/2(e))

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for session continuity, remembering preferences, ensuring site performance and security, anonymous analytics, and—where applicable—marketing/retargeting. You can change your cookie preferences at any time via the banner shown on first visit or your browser settings.

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for core functions and cannot be disabled.
  • Performance/Analytics: Produce anonymous statistics to improve the site.
  • Functional: Remember your preferences (language, theme, etc.).
  • Marketing: Used for advertising/retargeting where consent is given.

You may block/delete cookies in your browser; however, some features may be affected. To learn more, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Transfers to Third Parties and Data Processors

Your personal data may be transferred—only for the purposes stated in this policy and to the extent necessary—to the following recipient groups:

  • Hosting/CDN and infrastructure providers: e.g., hosting, content delivery network, firewall services.
  • Communication and form services: e.g., email delivery, contact form transmission services.
  • Analytics/Logging services: Performance, security, and usage statistics.
  • Competent authorities: As required by legal obligations and court/administrative orders.

Note: We do not sell your data. Processing activities are conducted with our processors under contracts and technical/organizational measures.

  • Hosting/CDN: Cloudflare
  • Form submission: Formspree
  • Font/asset delivery: Google Fonts (technical data such as IP and user‑agent may be processed upon request only)

10. International Data Transfers

Depending on server/provider locations, your personal data may be transferred to countries outside Türkiye. In such cases, an appropriate safeguard mechanism—adequacy decision, binding corporate rules, standard contractual clauses (SCCs), or a mechanism designated by the KVKK—will be applied; where necessary, your explicit consent will be obtained.

11. Retention Periods

Data is retained for the period necessary for the purposes of processing and as required by law; upon expiry, it is securely deleted, destroyed, or anonymized.

12. Security Measures

  • Appropriate encryption and transport security (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access control, authorization, and authentication processes
  • Least privilege principle and logging/monitoring
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability testing
  • Contractual and technical security measures with processors

In the event of any suspected personal data breach, an impact assessment is conducted; where necessary, notifications are made to individuals concerned and the competent authorities.

13. Artificial Intelligence, Profiling, and Automated Decisions

When developing our AI systems, we use anonymized/pseudonymized data wherever possible. We do not make decisions based solely on automated processing that produce legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affect you. Marketing activities based on profiling are conducted only with your consent, and you can opt out at any time.

14. Your Rights (KVKK Art. 11 and, where applicable, GDPR)

  • Learn whether your data is processed and request information
  • Learn whether your data is processed for its purpose and the third parties to whom it is transferred domestically/abroad
  • Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data
  • Request deletion/destruction under the conditions set by law
  • Request restriction of processing or object to processing
  • Object to automated processing and profiling
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Data portability (for users within the scope of the GDPR)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Authority/supervisory authority

15. Method of Application (Data Subject Requests)

You can submit your requests regarding your rights to info@briqmind.com. You must provide the information necessary for identity verification and clearly state your request. Applications are processed within 30 days as a rule.

16. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware of such collection, the data will be deleted within a reasonable time.
