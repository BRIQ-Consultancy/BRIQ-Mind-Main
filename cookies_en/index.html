Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

1. Introduction and Purpose

At BRIQ Mind ("BRIQ", "we"), we take the privacy and data security of visitors to our website (www.briqmind.com) very seriously. This Cookie Policy explains, in a transparent manner, which cookies we use on our site, why we use them, and how you can control these technologies. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

This policy is an integral part of our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

2. What Is a Cookie?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer, tablet, or mobile device when you visit a website. These files remember your preferences regarding your visit (such as language choice and session information) and help deliver a more personalized experience on your next visit. Cookies serve various functions, such as helping a site operate more efficiently and providing site owners with analytics insights.

3. Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies on our website for the following core purposes:

  • To Provide Basic Site Functionality: We use strictly necessary cookies to enable essential features such as secure navigation and form submissions.
  • To Improve Performance and User Experience: We use analytics cookies to analyze how our site is used (e.g., which pages are visited most) so that we can improve performance and deliver a smoother experience.
  • To Personalize and Market Our Services: We use marketing cookies to display content and ads that are more relevant to your interests and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Our site uses both first-party cookies (placed directly by us) and third-party cookies (placed by our partners). We categorize these cookies by their function as follows:

4.1. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are essential for the secure operation and basic functionality of our website. The legal basis is the establishment and performance of the service contract or our legitimate interests. They perform critical tasks such as managing user sessions, applying security measures, and network management. Your consent is not requested for these cookies and they cannot be disabled.

4.2. Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to collect anonymous information about how many people visit our site, how long they stay on certain pages, and which links they click. We use this data to measure the performance of our site, identify weaknesses, and continuously improve the overall user experience. The use of these cookies is subject to your explicit consent. If you do not accept these cookies, it will limit our ability to optimize site performance.

4.3. Marketing and Targeting Cookies

These cookies may be placed on our site by our advertising partners. They are used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other websites. They do not store personal information directly, but are based on your browser and internet device's unique identifiers. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.

6. How Can You Manage Your Cookie Preferences?

We offer several options to help you manage your cookie preferences.

6.1. Via Our Website

You can change your preferences at any time via the cookie notice panel that appears on your first visit or by clicking the "Manage cookies" link located in the footer of our website. In this panel, you can enable or disable cookie categories other than strictly necessary ones.

6.2. Via Browser Settings

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies automatically. However, you can change your browser settings to refuse cookies, accept cookies only from specific sites, or make your browser notify you when a cookie is stored. You can find instructions on how to change your browser settings at the links below:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

Please note that blocking all cookies via your browser may cause some features on our site to not function properly.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time due to changes in technology or legal regulations. We will announce significant changes on our website and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. By continuing to use our site after changes are made, you are deemed to have accepted the updated policy.
